Strawberry Balsamic Glazed Pork Chop Dinner

Description

For this strawberry balsamic glazed pork chop dinner, pork loin chops, baby potatoes, and asparagus roast on a sheet pan while a quick fresh strawberry sauce simmers on the stovetop. Savory and fruity, it's an elegant dinner with little effort.

Ingredients

  • cooking spray
  • 4 (3 to 4 ounce) boneless pork loin chops
  • 1 ½ pounds baby gold potatoes
  • 1 pound asparagus spears, trimmed
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • freshly cracked salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste
  • 2 cups fresh strawberries, hulled and chopped
  • ¼ cup balsamic vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons maple syrup
  • 2 teaspoons Dijon mustard
  • 1 pinch kosher salt

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Line a large sheet pan or jelly roll pan with aluminum foil and coat with nonstick cooking spray.

  2. Combine pork, potatoes, and asparagus in a large bowl. Toss with olive oil. Spread onto the sheet pan in an even layer with the vegetables around the pork chops.

  3. Sprinkle thyme over pork and vegetables; season with salt and pepper to taste.

  4. Bake in the preheated oven until the pork reaches an internal temperature of at least 145 degrees F (63 degrees C), and potatoes are fork tender, 20 to 22 minutes.

  5. Meanwhile, for glaze, combine strawberries, balsamic vinegar, maple syrup, Dijon, and salt in a small saucepan. Bring to a boil; stirring and mashing while cooking, then reduce heat. Simmer; continue to stir and mash berries occasionally, until berries are soft and the mixture is thick, 7 to 8 minutes.

  6. Serve pork chops with potatoes and asparagus. Spoon strawberry glaze over pork chops.
